Q4 2024 Performance Summary
Prepared by: Michael Chen, Chief Financial Officer

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Global Industries LLC achieved record revenue of $4,250,000.00 in Q4 2024,
representing a 12% increase over Q3 2024. Operating expenses were held to
$2,800,000.00, resulting in a net profit margin of 34%.

KEY FINANCIAL METRICS

Revenue: $4,250,000.00

Gross Profit: $2,550,000.00

Net Income: $1,450,000.00

OPERATIONAL HIGHLIGHTS

1. Product Development

The engineering team, led by David Park, successfully launched three new
product features ahead of schedule. Customer adoption rates exceeded
projections by 25%.

2. Sales Performance

The sales division, managed by Lisa Wang, closed 47 new enterprise contracts
with a total contract value of $3,200,000.00. Notable new clients include
Pacific Northwest Energy and Atlantic Shipping Corp.

3. Customer Success

Customer retention rate improved to 94%, up from 91% in Q3 2024.
Support ticket resolution time decreased by 18%.
